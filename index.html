Elisabeth Gray a repository of personal and academic work

I'm a documentarian by day and a grad student by night. I love cats, wine, food, and travel. I talk with my hands—especially when feeling enthusiastic—and generally read no less than three books at one time.

This site will be used to store academic papers and projects as well as observations leading up to my final research project for a Master of Science in Global Technology and Development at Arizona State University.
